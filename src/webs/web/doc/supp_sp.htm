If additional assistance is required, contact IPTC authorized Service Partner. In Sweden authorized Service Partner is BOSSIT AB www.bossit.se whom is qualified to provide a variety of services, including network planning, installation, hardware maintenance, application training, and support services.

When you contact Service Partner for assistance, have the following information ready:

  • Product model name, part number, and serial number

  • A list of system hardware and software, including revision levels

  • Diagnostic error messages (log files)

  • Details about any recent configuration changes

If you are unable to contact your network supplier, contact IPTC.
